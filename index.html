Welcome to the temperature and precipitation visualizer!

Description

This visualization identifies monthly temperature and precipitation trends for the selected city & year. The visualization uses data from Wunderground. To learn more about the columns in the dataset, scroll to the bottom of the page. Please note that the original scraped data has been aggregated to the month level.

Filters & Options







Dataset Columns

  • actual_mean_temp : The average of the daily measured temperatures for that month
  • actual_min_temp : The average of the daily measured minimum temperatures for that month
  • actual_max_temp : The average of the daily measured maximum temperature for that month
  • average_min_temp : The average of the minimum temperature since 1880 for each day in that month
  • average_max_temp : The average of the maximum temperature since 1880 for each day in that month
  • record_min_temp : The lowest ever temperature in that month since 1880
  • record_max_temp : The highest ever temperature in that month since 1880
  • actual_precipitation : The sum of the measured amount of rain or snow for that month
  • average_precipitation : The sum of the average amount of rain or snow for that month since 1880
  • record_precipitation : The highest amount of rain or snow a day for that month since 1880
