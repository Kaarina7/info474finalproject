Welcome to the temperature and precipitation visualizer!

Description

This visualization allows you to identify monthly temperature and precipitation trends for the selected city & year. The visualization uses data from Wunderground. If you want to learn more about the columns in the dataset, please scroll to the bottom of the page. Please note that the original scraped data has been aggregated to the month level.

Filters & Options







Dataset Columns

  • actual_mean_temp : The measured average temperature for that month
  • actual_min_temp : The measured minimum temperature for that month
  • actual_max_temp : The measured maximum temperature for that month
  • average_min_temp : The average minimum temperature for that month since 1880
  • average_max_temp : The average maximum temperature for that month since 1880
  • record_min_temp : The average of the lowest ever temperature for the days in that month since 1880
  • record_max_temp : The average of the highest ever temperature for the days in that month since 1880
  • actual_precipitation : The average measured amount of rain or snow for that month
  • average_precipitation : The average amount of rain or snow for that month since 1880
  • record_precipitation : The average of the highest amount of rain or snow for the days in that month since 1880
